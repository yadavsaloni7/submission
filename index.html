One-time Setup

To power the AI intake conversation, you need a free Anthropic API key. This is a one-time step — it's saved in your browser and never shared.

  1. 1
    Go to console.anthropic.com and create a free account.
  2. 2
    Click API Keys in the left sidebar, then Create Key.
  3. 3
    Copy the key (starts with sk-ant-) and paste it below.
Cost: ~$0.01–0.03 AUD per full intake conversation. Your key is stored only in your browser.

You can complete the AI intake later from your dashboard.
